# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in Agent Context Center to be a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. Maintainers and
contributors are expected to act in ways that keep the community open, welcoming,
and professional.

## Our standards

Contributors should:

- be considerate and respectful in language and conduct
- welcome differing viewpoints and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- focus on what is best for the project and its users
- assume good intent and give other people the benefit of the doubt

The following are not acceptable: harassment of any kind, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, or other
conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Standard adopted

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text,
including the detailed enforcement guidelines, is published at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>. By
participating, you agree to uphold it.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
and discussions — and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer privately through GitHub (open a
private report via the repository's **Security** tab, or contact
[@omerakben](https://github.com/omerakben)). Reports are handled discreetly.
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and other
activity that does not align with this code, and may temporarily or permanently
ban any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate.
